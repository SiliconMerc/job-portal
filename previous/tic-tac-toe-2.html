  • The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares.
  • You are X, your friend is O. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares and appoint square 3x3 for other player.
  • the players play to win or tie in sub 3x3 and to win bigger tic-tac-toe.
  • The first player to get 3 of her marks(tie and win combined together) in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally)in the bigger 3x3 is the winner.
  • When all 9 squares are full, the game is over. If no player has 3 marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
